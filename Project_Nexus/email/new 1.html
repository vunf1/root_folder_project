SUITS INC

Following the expansion in Spain

Store at Parque Nascente - Oporto - Portugal

The Portuguese brand Suits Inc keeps growing in order to become a brand reference in men fashion.

2016 is a good year for the brand in Spain where it has been opening in many cities such as: Madrid, Getafe, Jerez, Barcelona and Sevilla. The brand is also the sponsor of some soccer teams in Portugal: Futebol Clube do Porto, Sporting Clube de Portugal, Sporting de Braga and Vitória de Guimarães. In Spain it is the sponsor of the TV channel Real Madrid TV.

The investment in Spain has been important for local economy and also for employment. Each store is able to have between 5 to 7 employees. Each opening means an investment of around 200.000 Euros.

UNDER BLUE

Wants to conquer Spain and Latin America

Store at The Style Outet Vila do Conde - Portugal

The Portuguese brand Under Blue is unstoppable. Along with store openings in Spain and changing the image of the stores already opened, Under Blue is flying to Latin America.

Under Blue has just closed its first franchise contract outside Portugal, more precisely in Madrid, and they will open at Sambil outlet.

Under Blue is also the Sponsor of the Portuguese Football league.

2016 is a year of national and international investiment with international partnership.

The band worries about employment issues and that is why there are glad to be part of the group of companies that can help to overcome this situation.

Teddy Group

 

Teddy Group is an Italian group that has the following brands: Calliope, Terranova and Rinascimento. Their big store with good product at accessible prices is their way to success. 
Retail Mind is on the same way along with the group since March 2016 in order to find national and international partners for Iberian market, preparing new things for the beginning of the year 2017.
